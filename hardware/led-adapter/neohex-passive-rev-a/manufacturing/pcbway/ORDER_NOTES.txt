Prototype PCBA for NeoHEX passive adapter Rev A.

Please assemble top-side SMT parts only.

Populate:
- J2: JST PH S2B-PH-SM4-TB 2-pin SMT right-angle connector
- J3: JST SH BM04B-SRSS-TB 4-pin SMT connector
- J4: JST SH BM03B-SRSS-TB 3-pin SMT connector
- J5: JST SH BM04B-SRSS-TB 4-pin SMT connector
- R1: 330 ohm 0805 resistor
- C1: 0.1 uF 0805 ceramic capacitor

Do not populate:
- J1: M5Stack A118 / HY2.0-4P SMD Grove-style connector, DNP for this
  prototype packet. Please still fabricate the J1 pads from the Gerbers.
- C2: optional bulk capacitor, DNP for this prototype packet

Do not treat these as components:
- SJ1, SJ2, SJ3, SJ4 are solder jumpers in copper. Leave them open.
- TP1, TP2, TP3, TP4, TP5 are bare test pads.
- H1 and H2 are mounting holes.

Connector / orientation notes:
- J5 fallback output pinout: 1 GND, 2 VLED, 3 NC, 4 DATA.
- J1 and J5 are parallel output footprints. For this assembly, J1 is DNP and
  J5 is the intended output connector.
- Please verify connector rotation/polarity against silkscreen and
  pick-and-place data before assembly.

PCB enquiry counts:
- SMT pads total number: 46.
- Through-holes / drill holes: 14.
- Through-hole components to assemble: 0.
- SMD components to place from BOM: 6.

Known prototype caveats:
- J1 uses a local candidate footprint for the M5Stack A118 connector, but is
  omitted from assembly because sourcing it dominates cost and lead time.
- J5 is included as a fallback output for an Adafruit 4528-style
  Grove-to-STEMMA-QT cable.
- C2 is intentionally omitted to reduce prototype assembly sourcing friction.
