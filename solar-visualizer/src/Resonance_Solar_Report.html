Method & caveats

  • Sun position verified in-model: solar noon Sep 2 = 12:57 PDT, altitude 56.9°, shadow due north.
  • Direct-sun fraction = 15 ray tests across each panel's actual face toward the sun, every 15 minutes, all 10 days (≈125,000 rays). The weave is real strut geometry, so light passing through gaps is captured correctly.
  • Energy = clear-sky beam (Meinel model, +1.2% for 1,190m elevation) × cos(incidence) × lit fraction, plus sky-diffuse scaled by each panel's measured sky-view factor, plus playa-reflected (albedo 0.3). Numbers are for comparing positions/angles — real-world yield will be lower (heat, dust, panel losses).
  • "Full sun" = ≥14 of 15 face points lit. "Partial" = 1–13 points lit.
  • Angle recommendations searched 5° tilt × 15° azimuth steps at each panel's actual position with its actual shading.
  • The 4 door lights were deleted from the model and are not measured. TB-01 ENE and TB-07 WSW sit on the root-arm bulge at r≈3.8m — verify those two locations.
